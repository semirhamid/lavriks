About

Lavricks is an exclusive importer for popular snus brands like Killa, KIC, and Pablo, supporting a wide variety of high-quality nicotine pouches from Europe. Our mission is to bring top-quality products closer to our customers by providing unbeatable service and exceptional product knowledge.

Key product ranges include Killa and Pablo nicotine pouches—synonymous among snus aficionados for their strong flavors and smooth experiences. Whether you are a seasoned snus enthusiast or new to the world of nicotine pouches, our curated selections offer something for everyone.

Other product ranges distributed throughout the UK include heavy snus, beverages, and more—continuously expanding to meet the demand for unique products.

If you're interested in joining our distribution network or simply want to learn more about these products, feel free to reach out via the contact form below or browse our extensive product listings.
